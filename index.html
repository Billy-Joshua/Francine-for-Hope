    About Us

    Our mission in Rwanda and Africa.

    Francine for Hope is a beacon of support and empowerment for cancer patients and their families in Rwanda and across Africa. Inspired by the founder's profound loss of their mother to lung, liver, and colon cancer, our mission is to provide accessible resources, financial aid for treatments, transportation, and nutrition, while fostering awareness and community connections. We partner with local institutions like Butaro Cancer Center and Rwanda Cancer Center to bridge gaps in care, ensuring no one faces this battle alone. Through education, innovation, and compassion, we aim to turn pain into purpose and build a future where hope triumphs over cancer.
